World's first open source free space optical communication system Koruza

With KORUZA you can connect buildings up to 100 m apart with the link capacity of 1 Gbps or 10Gbps.

The system uses collimated infrared light beam, which is completely eye safe.

KORUZA provides an alternative to WIFI networks in urban areas, which suffer from high RF spectrum congestion.

With our system you can build large networks or just share the connection with your neighbour.

It is a solution for all the locations lacking other options.

KORUZA is a price friendly solution installed in less than a day for:
